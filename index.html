LOKDWN Privacy Policy

LOKDWN is a focus application designed to block selected apps during active lockdown sessions.

Data Collection

LOKDWN does not collect personal data such as messages, contacts, browsing history, or screen content.

The app stores the following locally on the device:

  • Total lockdown time completed
  • Override usage count
  • Selected app preferences

Accessibility Usage

LOKDWN uses Android Accessibility Service solely to detect when a blocked application is launched during an active session and to display a blocking screen. No content is read, stored, or transmitted.

Data Sharing

LOKDWN does not sell or share personal information.
